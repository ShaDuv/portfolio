Hobbies and Interests

My hobbies and interests are as varied as everything else in my life. I love to travel and when I do I try to take advantage of being an advanced open water Scuba diver since the water on the Pacific coast is too cold for my tastes. I occasionally do archery which is noted by the bruises on my forearm; just because I like it doesn’t mean I am good at it yet. Theater is a passion of mine that I have not had time for in recent years. Doing improvisational comedy (“Whose Line Is it Anyway” style) was some of the happiest times in my life. I am a board game geek, a dog lover, a non-picky self-labeled wine snob, a coffee drinker, a sushi eater and a total water baby. Finally, I am fascinated by psychology and the way humans think and act. I am usually studying some form of psychology and I am looking to push more into ways to educate people on human sexuality and the way our psyche plays a part in that.

Education

Being a curious creature I have a degreen in Graphic Design from Westwood Univerity (2011), a certification in Advanced Hypnosis and Neuro-Linguistic Programming, and soon a certification in Ruby and React. I find learning new things to be one of the real pleasures in life. If I am not challenged I am bored. Reading books on human behavior, psychology, and business tends to carry me between these bigger learning pushes I put myself through.

Experience

Much like my learning my experience has been extremely varied. Most employers move me up and through various positions when they realize that I am an extremely fast learner, not afraid of change, a unique problem solver, and self-motivated. I have started with 6 months in the basement followed by a view from the penthouse and within a year I am some form of a strategist. I like the challenge and I love growing and seeing things improve. Efficiency is like a drug to me and I am often looking for ways to automate or shortcut unnecessary steps.
